Omnia Amethyste

Graceful Elegance

Inspired by the shimmering hues of amethyst, the floral Omnia Amethyste is the expression of an elegant sensuality and extreme femininity. A dew-drenched blend of rose and iris creating a noble and graceful scent.

A sophisticated, nuanced bouquet

A fruity opening

In the opening, the delicate fruity accents of pink grapefruit and green sap carry the pure perfume of spring.

A luxurious heart

The fragrance's central theme is the noble Iris, a flower of great luxury and originality, blended with the fresh notes of exquisite Bulgarian rosebuds.

A sensual dry down

The unmistakable accents of heliotrope and vibrant solar wood infuse the base notes with warmth and sensuality.
